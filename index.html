The Practice — 01

A studio of
two decades.

— Founded by Jerry Thomas, 2002

Architecture as discipline.

T&T Design & Development has been registered as an active Florida corporation since 29 April 2002 — more than two decades of commercial, industrial, and mixed-use work delivered across Broward County.

We work closely with our clients and take on a small number of projects each year. Most of our work comes from referrals — from developers, owners, general contractors, and municipalities we have worked with for years. Our signature project, the 75,000 square-foot Flora Fine Foods headquarters, opened in 2021 in partnership with Butters Construction.

Each engagement is led by a principal, not handed off. The drawings should come from the people doing the work, and the buildings should come from the drawings.

Methodology — 05

How we work.

M 01

Discover the site

We visit in person, review entitlements, study the surrounding fabric, and listen to what you need the building to do.

M 02

Draw, test, refine

Schematic design, concept imagery, and early coordination with civil and structural consultants. We rule out what does not work.

M 03

Document the build

Construction documents coordinated with engineers, specifications, and submittal packages prepared for municipal review.

M 04

Administer construction

Field visits, RFI responses, shop drawing review, and punch-list walkthroughs until the work meets the drawings.
